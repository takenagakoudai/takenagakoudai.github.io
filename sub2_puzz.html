Alexandrov Puzzle

This puzzle can be used to realize any convex polyhedron that can be generated by edge gluing to the natural 14 edges of the Latin cross, provided that the folds are well chosen.

The name of this puzzle is Alexandrov Puzzle, because it was inspired by Alexandrov's theorem.

I am hoping to commercialize it.
